Five talented stylists, one shared passion — making you look and feel extraordinary.

Hairdresser 1
Founder & Creative Director

With over 15 years in the industry, our founder brings a rare blend of technical mastery and artistic vision to every appointment. Trained in London and further refined in Paris, she leads Morgan Lloyd Hair with warmth, precision, and an unwavering commitment to craft.

Her speciality lies in lived-in colour and transformative cuts that work with your natural texture, not against it.
